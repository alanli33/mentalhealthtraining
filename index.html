Strategic Framework

Mental Health Training Initiative (2026)

📋 Executive Overview

This proposal presents a phased roll-out strategy for our upcoming mental health training initiatives. By structuring the deployment into two distinct operational stages, we minimize initial market risk while building up to a scaled, high-exposure international program.

  • Stage 1 (Test Market): A hyper-targeted internal launch focusing on the GOLD community to validate operational mechanics and local reception.
  • Stage 2 (International Exposure): A scaled, public-facing medical program leveraging dual-institution prestige across the Greater Bay Area.

This initial stage serves as a controlled pilot program designed to establish operational benchmarks, gather direct community feedback, and test the localized delivery format.

🗺️ Flexible Collaboration Tracks

To accommodate diverse regional regulatory frameworks, the center will offer two distinct participation tracks:

  • Option A (Mainland China Exclusive Tour): A dedicated, closed 1-day public cohort hosted at a premium venue within Mainland China.
  • Option B (Hong Kong Hub Referral): Full program execution managed directly at the Hong Kong core campus, with our team acting as the primary mainland marketing and recruitment pipeline on a commission/referral basis.

💳 Investment Structure

Standard Public Rate: RMB 2,000 per participant (to be discussed)

🏅 High-Tier Certification & Program Deliverables

Graduates will receive a Certificate of Completion issued jointly by all hosting entities, including but not limited to Stanford University, The Hong Kong Mental Wellness Association, The Alpha Wellbeing Centre and GBA-FM-ARC. This certification validates the participant's competency in cutting-edge neuro-psychiatric interventions and provides formal academic curriculum recognition based on frameworks established by Stanford University. Additionally, participating medical professionals will gain access to an exclusive alumni network for ongoing clinical case discussions and cross-border medical referrals.

战略方案

心理健康培训计划 (2026)

📋 执行摘要

本提案介绍了即将开展的心理健康培训计划的分阶段实施战略。通过将计划分为两个不同的运营阶段，我们在将初期市场风险降至最低的同时，逐步打造一个具有广泛影响力的大型国际化医疗培训项目。

  • 第一阶段（市场测试）： 聚焦GOLD社区的内部精准发布，旨在验证运营机制及本地市场反馈。
  • 第二阶段（国际影响力）： 面向公众的大型医疗培训项目，依托大湾区双机构的权威声誉。

🗺️ 灵活的合作路径

为适应不同地区的监管框架，中心将提供两种不同的参与路径：

  • 选项 A（内地专场）： 在粤港澳大湾区核心城市（优选深圳市）举办的1天互动工作坊。
  • 选项 B（香港转介）： 培训项目由香港举办方直接管理和执行，我们团队作为内地主要的营销和招募渠道，以收取佣金/转介费的模式合作。

🏅 高级别认证与项目交付成果

毕业生将获得由所有主办实体联合颁发的结业证书，包括但不限于斯坦福大学、香港情绪健康学会、恩德贤医务中心以及深圳市粤港澳大湾区联合家庭医学评审评价研究中心（GBA-FM-ARC）。此认证验证了参与者在尖端神经精神干预方面的胜任能力，并提供了基于斯坦福大学所建立框架的正式学术课程认可。此外，参与的医疗专业人员将获准加入专属社群网络，以进行持续的临床病例讨论和跨境医疗转介。
